About

 

Welcome to my web page! I'm delighted to have caught your attention and that you've taken in interest in my coding skills.

I’m a graduated Electrical Engineer with work experience in the Oil & Gas industry who recently decided to make a career change into Information Technology. After travelling the world for almost four years dealing with people with different backgrounds and also working in different fields, I realized coding is an invaluable skill to have for many years to come.

Problem solving was always something I enjoyed doing and it eventually became part of my daily life as an engineer and a traveller. With the passion for new technologies and the belief in building great thing that can improve people’s lives and businesses in hand, changing my career path to Information Technology was not only a necessity but also a natural process.

I’m a self-taught Web Developer student currently building strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React.js not to mention my previous C++ experience thanks to my days as an engineer. Feel free to check some of my work below and shoot me an email under Contact section..
