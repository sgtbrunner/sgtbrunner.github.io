About

 

Welcome to my web page! I'm delighted to have caught your attention and that you've taken in interest in my coding skills.

I’m a graduated Electrical Engineer with work experience in the Oil & Gas industry who decided to make a career change into Web Development. After travelling the world for almost four years dealing with people with different backgrounds and also working in different fields, I realized coding is an invaluable skill to have for many years to come.

Problem solving was always something I enjoyed doing and it eventually became part of my daily life as an engineer and a traveller. With the passion for new technologies and the belief in building great thing that can improve people’s lives and businesses in hand, changing my career path to Information Technology was not only a necessity but also a natural process.

I’m a self-taught Web Developer with strong skills in Front-End technologies like HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript and React.js and familiar with Back-End technologies such as Restful Api's, Node.js, Express.js and PostgreSQL not to mention my skills in tools like Git & GitHub, NPM and Agile & Scrum. Feel free to check some of my work below and shoot me an email under Contact section if you'd like to work together.
